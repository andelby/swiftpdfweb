Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

SwiftPDF is a PDF viewer application for Windows. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how SwiftPDF handles your data.

Data Collection

SwiftPDF processes all PDF files locally on your device. We do not:

  • Upload your PDF files to any server
  • Send your documents to external services
  • Collect personal information from users
  • Track your viewing habits or document history

Local Processing

All PDF rendering, annotation, and editing happens entirely on your local machine. Your documents never leave your device during normal use.

Office Document Conversion

When opening Microsoft Office documents (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), SwiftPDF converts them locally using the Microsoft Office interop libraries. No data is transmitted externally during this process.

Crash Reporting

SwiftPDF may collect anonymous crash reports to help improve the application. These reports contain:

  • Error messages and stack traces
  • Application version information
  • Basic system information (OS version, architecture)

No personal documents, file contents, or personally identifiable information is included in crash reports.

Third-Party Services

SwiftPDF uses the following third-party components:

  • WebView2 Runtime - Microsoft's embedded web platform for PDF rendering
  • PDF.js - Mozilla's JavaScript PDF library (runs locally in WebView2)

These components are subject to their respective privacy policies.

Data Storage

SwiftPDF stores:

  • Application settings - Stored locally in your user profile
  • Window positions - Remembered between sessions
  • Recent files list - Stored locally and never synced

Permissions

SwiftPDF requires the following Windows permissions:

  • File access - To open and save PDF files you choose
  • Network access - Required by WebView2 runtime for local processing

Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.

Your Rights

Since all data is processed locally on your device:

  • You retain full control over your documents
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • You can uninstall SwiftPDF at any time to remove all local data
